Welcome to our Charity foundation.


In 2020 mudiba foundation was formed to address the gap in rural areas about hunger and literacy during covid 19 pandemic and that provided support for children, elderly and needy families.
We have been able to change and save number's of lives from hunger in Eastern parts of Uganda.


Our contribution to society :



  • MUDIBA CHARITY FOUNDATION rescues vulnerable people in the society and places them in loving families. Our goal is not only to meet their physical needs, but to give them everything they need to become healthy and whole, and live a life of purpose.

  • When you sponsor a a vulnerable person, you help us provide food, clothing, medical care, education and a safe place they can call home. Each person can have multiple sponsors in order to give them the holistic care that every person needs to thrive in life.

  • No one deserve to sleep hungry in the world we my not feed everyone in the world but trying to feed everyone in the world is a honorable act. 



  • Education

    We support the most vulnerable children through education, helping them to get the most from their time at school and providing a foundation for a productive and happy future

  • Health, Nutrition and Social Care

    We provide support and advice on health, nutrition and social care to children, their families and the wider community, equipping them with the knowledge and means to lead a safe and healthy life.

  • Alternative Education and Livelihoods

    We empower young people, parents and guardians with the education, skills and resources needed to take up productive livelihoods and employment opportunities within their own communities.
